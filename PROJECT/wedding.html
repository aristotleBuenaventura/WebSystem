Wedding Events

Where the adventure begins

Ready to start a relationship with our historical and cultural auditorium all over again. We have a variety of stunning wedding venues to choose from, ranging from big to small. We have the ideal atmosphere for your special occasion in a range of styles. The same exceptional service also accompanies the historic venues that our landmark hotel is recognized for.

Large wedding celebrations have taken place at the Gourmet Hotel. With its ambiance of classical beauty, the great dame is the epitome of elegance and luxury and is known for its legendary service and Filipino friendliness. The hotel's staff's warmth and service excellence will exceed your expectations and ensure the success of your reception.

Appreciate an award-winning culinary team's wedding meal, evening snacks, and a variety of special extras. Our popular all-inclusive packages offer a cost-effective option without sacrificing quality. We have a variety of amenities to make your wedding particularly unique, as well as tasty cuisine options.

We invite you to share your special day with us since we have a wealth of experience and a reputation for style. Our spectacular hotel, located in the center of Manila, allows you to construct the wedding day of your dreams with the freedom and originality you desire.
