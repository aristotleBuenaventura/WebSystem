About the Company

The Hotel's History.

Gourmet Hotel started during the pandemic. The founders established the company to provide rooms to foreigners or OFWs that arrived in the country.

The Gourmet Hotel is located just minutes from the heart of Quezon City. Conveniently situated away from congestion and crowds, we provide you with a refreshing opportunity to experience choices and flexibility like never before. Complimentary high-speed Internet is available throughout our hotel, and keep up your physical routine in our 24-hour fitness center. Our spacious guest rooms provide flexibility in your workspace and comfort in your rest space. Whether traveling for business or pleasure, your stay at the Gourmet Hotel is sure to be more comfortable, productive, and enjoyable than ever before.
